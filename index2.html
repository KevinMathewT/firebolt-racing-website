About Firebolt

We are Team Firebolt Racing, the Official Off Road Racing Team of Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. Team consists of 30 automobile enthusiasts from different engineering spheres who are guided by a highly efficient Faculty Advisor with a common goal of designing and manufacturing an all terrain vehicle. We are currently the best team in East India and one among top 15 teams of the country. Our works put into use the theoretical concepts in a practical environment along with balancing the monetary and design requirements.
